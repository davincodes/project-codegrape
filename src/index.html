CODEGRAPE

A Deep-Learning Based Image Recognition System for Automated Detection of Grape Leaf Diseases.

SERVICE

I have developed a web-based tool powered by Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) to assist grape farmers and viticulturists in diagnosing grape leaf diseases. The platform allows users to upload images of their grape leaves and receive an accurate diagnosis. With an intuitive interface, the tool makes it easy to upload, classify, and access information about potential diseases.

After selecting a photo, the system promptly analyzes the image to identify any diseases affecting the grape crop. It then provides a detailed description of the identified disease, explaining its characteristics and potential impact on crop yield and quality. Additionally, the tool suggests possible treatments based on current research and expert recommendations.
